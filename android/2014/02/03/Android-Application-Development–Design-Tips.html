Android Application Development – Design Tips

As an android developer, we must think before start working on design of an application. It is very much important to think in regards of target audience / actual users of application while we are designing interfaces / layouts.

Here are some useful design tips:

Use objects instead of buttons and menus

Let user personalize the way they want

Show intelligence in learning people’s prefernces rather asking them every time

Keep it short and sweet for users to follow instructions

User pictures at places instead of words, it impacts more

Suggest user the best possible action, but let him pick final decision with other possible alternatives

No extra stuff to be shown to user when he is looking for specific thing

Let user be aware about where exactly he is, while he is navigating between different apps

Remember user’s personal settings and creations for future usage, they are pleased to have such feature

Avoid modes, which are places that look similar but act differently on the same input

Avoid interrupting people (for un important alerts) in the middle of what they are focusing on

People feel great when they figure things out for themselves

Instead of just a message or warning, give correct recovery instructions

Break complex tasks into smaller steps that can be easily accomplished.

Do the heavy lifting for user, to make them feel expert about certain things that they never thought before

Make important things performs fast and brings quicker response

For more detailed information, please refer: http://developer.android.com/design/get-started/principles.html

I hope this post will be helpful in designing android application, enjoy designing!
